Frequently Asked Questions

How do I upload documents for analysis? +

Simply go to the Home page and click "Get Started". Then you can upload Word documents (.docx) or PDF files. Our system will automatically process them and provide sentiment analysis results.

What file formats do you support? +

We currently support Microsoft Word documents (.docx) and PDF files. We're working on adding support for more formats in future updates.

How accurate is your sentiment analysis? +

Our sentiment analysis is about 85-90% accurate for general text. Accuracy may vary depending on the complexity of the text and domain-specific language.

Can I analyze multiple documents at once? +

Yes! With our Pro and Enterprise plans, you can upload multiple documents at once and get batch analysis results.
